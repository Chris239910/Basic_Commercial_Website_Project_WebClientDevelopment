While the runway showcases what’s coming next in fashion, the streets display not only the future but also what’s on-trend right now. From Paris and Milan to London and New York, every fashion week from the month featured outstanding outfits and lust-worthy looks. Here, we’ve rounded up the top trends from the season for you to add to your shopping list.
